Orientation for Moving
#organizer: Tanaka & Fumi
#date-and-time: 2012-12-20 14:45 to  18:00 and 2012-12-26 10:00 to 12:00
#place: CALE Forum, 2F ECIS/CALE Bldg.
#email: adv@ecis.nagoya-u.ac.jp

For those who need to move in March, it is time to gather information
for finding a new accommodation.  We will give general information
about housing and moving. A similar orientation will be held in June
or July of next year.

@@DETAILS@@

To register, please send an E-mail giving your name and school,
together with any questions you may have, to [adv@ecis.nagoya-u.ac.jp](mailto:adv@ecis.nagoya-u.ac.jp)

This event is coordinated by ECIS Advising & Counselling Services (Room #204, ECIS Building).

A [flier about the event](http://www.ecis.nagoya-u.ac.jp/exchange/2012dec-moving.pdf)
is available, as well as [a pamphlet](http://www.ecis.nagoya-u.ac.jp/info/life/moving2012.pdf).

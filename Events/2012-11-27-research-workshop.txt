Research methods workshop: Boren Kork and Ouach Tri (through 30 Nov [Fri]).

Dr. Boren Kork and Mr. Ouach Tri while lead a research methods
workshop on November 29th and 30th. The session is made possible
with support from the JDS program. All students are welcome to
attend. The lecturers will speak from their own experience,
offering advice on the research process, thesis writing, and
student life. Their presentation will be followed by a question
and answer session.
 
> **Date:** 9:00-12:00, 27-30 Nov (Tue to Fri)
>
> **Place:** Integrated Research Building, Room 408
> 
> **Title:** Research Methods Workshop
>
> **Presenters:** Boren Kork and Ouach Tri

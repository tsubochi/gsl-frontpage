Nagoya at the Intercollegiate Negotiation Competition

Nagoya University will participate in the [Eleventh
Intercollegiate Negotiation Competition](http://www.osipp.osaka-u.ac.jp/inc/eng/index.html) to be held
at Sophia University on December 1 & 2 this year.

The Competition (known fondly to its alumni as
"NegoComp") is made possible through generous sponsorship
by the Sumitomo Group Public Relations Committee, White & Case,
the Japan Chapter of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators,
the Japan Association of Arbitrators, and the Japanese Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs.

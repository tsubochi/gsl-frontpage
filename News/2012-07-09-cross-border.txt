Cross-Border Education Programs
The Faculty of Law has initiated two new programs for
cross-border education in law and politics:

> **Campus Asia:** An undergraduate exchange program 
> in partnership with universities in China and Korea.
> Details are available on the [Campus Asia project website](http://www.law.nagoya-u.ac.jp/project/en/Asia/index.html).
>
> **Leading Graduate Schools:** A graduate degree
> program focused on cross-border collaboration in
> legal institution design and implementation. Details
> are available on the [Leading Graduate Schools project website](http://www.law.nagoya-u.ac.jp/project/en/Leading/index.html).

These two new initiatives are coordinated through the
[Cross-Border Projects Office](http://www.law.nagoya-u.ac.jp/project/en/).
